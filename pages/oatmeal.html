Oatmeal

Enjoy this Oatmeal recipe for every morning!

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup milk (or water)
  • ½ cup fresh strawberries (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp honey/maple syrup
  • 1 tsp chia seeds (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Preparations:

  1. Cook oats: In a saucepan, boil milk/water, add oats, and simmer for 5 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  2. Sweeten: Add honey and salt.
  3. Serve: Top with strawberries and chia seeds.
