Cookies

Enjoy this Cookies recipe for a sweet day!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ cup butter (softened)
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Preparations:

  1. Mix dry: Whisk flour and baking soda.
  2. Cream butter & sugars: Beat until fluffy. Add egg and vanilla..
  3. Combine: Gradually mix dry ingredients into wet. Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Bake: Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 minutes.
