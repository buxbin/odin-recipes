Pizza Margherita

Enjoy these Pizza recipe for the weekend!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 ½ cups fresh mozzarella (sliced or shredded)
  • 5-6 fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Preparations:

  1. Dough: Roll out the dough on a floured surface to a 12-inch circle.
  2. Sauce: Spread tomato sauce evenly, leaving a small border for the crust.
  3. Cheese: Add mozzarella slices.
  4. Bake: Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
  5. Finish: Drizzle with olive oil, add basil leaves, and sprinkle salt.
